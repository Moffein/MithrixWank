## MithrixWank

There's too much ProvidenceWank in the lore! Providence this, Providence that! What happened to his brother?

This mod aims to add content that will spread Mithrix's influence across the planet.

Not sure if this will be a one-off, or if it will be something that expands over time.

## Shrine of Fealty

*After the fall of The Bulwark, the followers of the King of Nothing have grown bolder and more numerous.*

- Guaranteed spawn on Stage 5, lets you trade Lunar items/equipments in exchange for items.
	- Costs 20 director credits.

## Lunar Golems on Stage 5

*Each day, the King of Nothing comes closer to finally reclaiming his rightful throne on the planet.*

- Lunar Golems now spawn on Sky Meadow and Helminth Hatchery, and will be hostile towards other mobs.
